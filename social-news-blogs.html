Oak Grove Technologies makes donation to Flat Branch Volunteer Fire Department

Submitted by ingrid.sobrino on October 21, 2016 - 11:08am

In the wake of the destruction and devastation caused by Hurricane Matthew, Oak Grove Technologies presented a check on Wednesday, October 19, 2016 for one thousand five hundred dollars to Fire Department Training Officer, Lt. Christopher Anderson, in support of the Flat Branch Volunteer Fire Department Christmas Fund for 2016. During the informal ceremony at the Flat Branch Volunteer Fire Department located at 2098 Elliott Bridge Road in Bunnlevel, North Carolina, Oak Grove Technologies representative

Chuck Anderson, thanked the firefighters for their outstanding efforts in rescuing and airmen of the Special Tactics Squadron in how animals can assist in the accomplishment of theirk Grove Technologies Tactical Training Site, 158 Rushing Road, Hoffman North Carolina. The focus of the course was to present proven packing and riding skills to the soldiers of the 30th Armored Brigade Combat Team.
